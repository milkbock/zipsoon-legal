Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

ZipSoon Diary ("the App") values your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address via Google Sign-In
  • Service Usage: Credit balance, plan information

Your diary entries, mood records, and generated illustrations are stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • User identification and service delivery
  • Credit management and payment processing
  • Service improvement and error resolution

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following external services:

  • Google: Sign-in authentication
  • Google Gemini AI: Illustration generation (diary text is sent when generating images)
  • Supabase: Account and credit data storage

We do not share your personal information with third parties beyond the purposes described above.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Account data is deleted immediately upon account deletion.
  • Deleted account emails are stored in hashed form to prevent free credit abuse.
  • Diary data stored on your device remains after account deletion and is removed when the app is uninstalled.

5. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account at any time from the app settings.
  • All personal data stored on our servers is deleted upon account deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 14 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will notify you through the app or this page.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries: zipsoondiary@gmail.com
